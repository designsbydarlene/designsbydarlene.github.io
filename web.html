Trade Show-Stopper

"Trade Show-Stopper." is a web design created for presentation at the Nationwide Marketing Group semi-annual tradeshow. Our team had recently added Electrolux, Profile, and Vintec appliance brands, and this site served as a showcase for how dealers and clients could market these products to their customers. I focused on clean layouts, bold imagery, and clear messaging to help convey product quality and versatility in a professional, approachable way.
