Designing for Growth

This project is a complete UX/UI redesign for a local tree service company looking to modernize their online presence. The goal was to create a clean, intuitive experience that builds trust and makes it easy for users to request services, learn about offerings, and navigate with ease. The design features a streamlined layout, refreshed visual identity, and improved content hierarchy—all optimized for mobile responsiveness. Best Of Award 2024

This website earned the "Design of the Year" award in 2024 at my agency, standing out as the top project among a talented team of eight designers. Voted on by the entire company—including leadership, marketing, and development teams—this project was recognized for its innovation, impact, and exceptional attention to detail. The design successfully balanced visual storytelling with usability, setting a new standard for our team’s creative output. It remains one of the most celebrated pieces in my portfolio and a defining moment in my design career.

Bookings On The Go

GotSport Travel is a sub-application of the GotSport platform that helps teams, parents, and coaches easily book hotel accommodations for tournaments. Originally designed with desktop users in mind, the mobile experience lacked usability and clarity. This project focused on rethinking the mobile interface to prioritize accessibility, ease of booking, and clear navigation. The redesign introduces streamlined flows, mobile-first layouts, and intuitive touch interactions—making travel coordination simple, even on the go.
