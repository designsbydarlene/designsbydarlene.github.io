About.

With 9 years of hands-on experience in design and technology, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to every project I take on. My background spans a wide range of industries—from beauty and fashion to technology, home services, and non-profit organizations—allowing me to design with empathy, versatility, and purpose. I specialize in web, graphic, UX/UI, and multimedia design, with a strong foundation in both traditional front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and modern content management systems like WordPress.

In 2024, I was honored to receive the Design of the Year award at my agency—voted on by an 8-person design team and company-wide staff—recognizing both creative excellence and impact. I’ve created user-centered experiences for everything from startups and small businesses to established agencies and enterprise clients. In addition to my design expertise, I hold a Master’s Degree in Computer Science and have taught programming in Java and Python, blending technical depth with creative problem-solving. This portfolio serves as a snapshot of my work—crafted with intention, and ready for what’s next.

Holiday Cheer

A festive social media animation designed for the holiday season, featuring a stylized wreath in motion. This piece blends brand colors with a touch of seasonal warmth, creating an engaging and on-brand visual to spread cheer across digital platforms.

Animate To Impress

A conceptual presentation design for GotSport, created as a test project to showcase how animation can elevate corporate storytelling. The layout, pacing, and transitions were tailored to reflect the company’s energetic, tech-forward identity while maintaining professionalism and clarity.

Waves Of Technology

An animated gif designed for Network 2000, a tech support company based in Honolulu. The visual merges the iconic Waikiki coastline with abstract technology motifs—like glowing network veins—to symbolize the seamless integration of innovation and island life.

The Purr-fect Handyman

Created as a mascot for an HVAC and plumbing brand, The Purr-fect Handyman brings a playful, approachable face to home services. This character was designed to build brand trust and memorability, using a clever twist on the “handyman” persona while still conveying professionalism and technical reliability.
