Trade Show-Stopper

Designed a timeless logo for White Rose Furniture, a newly established, family-owned furniture store. The branding captures the elegance and approachability of their handcrafted offerings, with a soft yet structured aesthetic that reflects both tradition and modern comfort. This logo served as the foundation for their visual identity across digital and print.

Best Of Award 2024

This website earned the "Design of the Year" award in 2024 at my agency, standing out as the top project among a talented team of eight designers. Voted on by the entire company—including leadership, marketing, and development teams—this project was recognized for its innovation, impact, and exceptional attention to detail. The design successfully balanced visual storytelling with usability, setting a new standard for our team’s creative output. It remains one of the most celebrated pieces in my portfolio and a defining moment in my design career.

Luxurious Appliance Design

This luxury appliance website for Compton & Son Appliance introduced a custom brand slider concept that I’m especially proud of. Designed to elevate the user experience, the slider seamlessly showcases high-end appliance brands while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic. The layout was tailored to reflect the sophistication of the products, using refined typography, spacious design, and rich imagery. This project reflects my dedication to blending usability with bold visual concepts, and the brand slider has since become a standout feature in my portfolio.
