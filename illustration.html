Designing for Growth

This project is a complete UX/UI redesign for a local tree service company looking to modernize their online presence. The goal was to create a clean, intuitive experience that builds trust and makes it easy for users to request services, learn about offerings, and navigate with ease. The design features a streamlined layout, refreshed visual identity, and improved content hierarchy—all optimized for mobile responsiveness. A Bold Beauty

This independent digital artwork is a personal expression of my creativity and love for visual storytelling. With a background in the beauty industry, I often draw inspiration from bold femininity, fashion, and elegance. A Bold Beauty blends those influences into a stylized piece that reflects both my artistic roots and my design evolution.

Paisley Power

Paisley Power continues my exploration of digital illustration with a vibrant, expressive approach. Drawing from my years in the beauty industry, this piece combines intricate pattern work with personality-driven portraiture. It’s a celebration of color, individuality, and the artistry that lives at the intersection of beauty and design.

The Purr-fect Handyman

Created as a mascot for an HVAC and plumbing brand, The Purr-fect Handyman brings a playful, approachable face to home services. This character was designed to build brand trust and memorability, using a clever twist on the “handyman” persona while still conveying professionalism and technical reliability.
